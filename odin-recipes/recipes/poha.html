Poha Recipe

Description

Poha (flattened rice) is typically a Western Indian breakfast recipe but it’s taste and ease of making has made it extremely popular all over India. Poha is very easy to make, it requires almost no cooking, the flattened rice just need to be rinsed till it’s softened and that’s about it. You don’t need to cook it like any other grain. Traditionally in the state of Maharasthra, it is known as “kanda poha” (kanda=onion) where poha is made with only onions and I prefer that version too.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cup poha
  • 1 onion
  • 1 potato
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3/4tbsp jira
  • 1 pinch hing
  • 1 tbsp coriander
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps/Instructions

  1. Rinse quickly with water & drain water completely
  2. Pour oil to the same pan & add cumin
  3. Cook on a very low heat until the poha turns hot
  4. Add coriander leaves & turn off heat
  5. Garnish poha with roasted peanuts & coriander leaves
